FAQ for our services


How does this work?

Essentially, you submit a clip, our AI check it for offensive content, copyrighted content, etc and if there is none, it will post on the channel!

What difference does it make if I buy a server or not?

The way our bot (better said, ai) works is that it compiles clips from many different people. Which means there is a chance your clip isn't seen. People also buy the server so that they can upload videos that use only their clips. (this doesn't affect the videos that use shared clips and how they upload.)

Why does the 'dedicated server' option cost so much?

Our service (admittedly) work on raspberry pis. More specifically the Raspberry PI 4.
These are pretty costly at a pricing of almost $50 in Canadian Dollar. (We are a canadian service, yes.)
The reason why we ask at a price of $60 is a chance to support the people behind setting up the servers. Really, you're paying $50 for the server and $10 for a setup fee.

API Access?! What's that?

We're working hard to provide a way for you to integrate our service in your projects. It's as easy as 1, 2, 3!
You make a reference to our service and provide a few arguments and that's it! More on that in our Docs.
